System Capabilities

Integrated compliance with high-precision positioning.

Since January 1, 2024, the Panama Canal Authority requires a fixed Non-Portable Piloting Unit (NPPU) with Real-Time Kinematics (RTK) for centimeter-level accuracy. CanalNav-VR eliminates the transit delays and reliability problems of portable solutions.
